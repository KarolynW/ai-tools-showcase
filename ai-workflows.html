AI Workflows Supporting Graduate Capabilities

This document outlines how creative AI workflows can help develop UK graduate capabilities through active engagement with tools and processes, rather than via direct assessment. It aligns each capability area with concrete, accessible examples of AI use in higher education contexts.

1. Academic & Intellectual Skills

Examples: Critical thinking, synthesis, enquiry, argumentation

AI Workflows for Academic Thinking
Workflow Tools How it helps
Socratic prompting ChatGPT, Claude Practice structured debate and counterarguments with AI as a thought partner
Semantic research synthesis Elicit, Notebook LM Support independent research through AI-augmented search and summarisation
Concept map co-creation LucidSpark + GPT Generate, critique and iterate academic concepts and connections visually

2. Communication Skills

Examples: Written/verbal clarity, audience awareness, presentation

AI Workflows for Communication Development
Workflow Tools How it helps
Rewrite for tone and clarity ChatGPT, Grammarly Practice writing for different audiences and improving structure
Script to voice-over ElevenLabs, Adobe Express Transform written ideas into multimodal presentations
Blog and content co-authoring Notion AI, Ghostwriter Develop concise, audience-focused content creation skills

3. Digital & Information Literacy

Examples: Tool fluency, source evaluation, data awareness

AI Workflows for Digital Literacy
Workflow Tools How it helps
Cross-check AI outputs ChatGPT + Google/Perplexity Develop habits of verification and healthy digital scepticism
Prompt engineering practice Custom AI templates Teaches students how to query AI effectively and ethically
Interactive data exploration Excel Copilot, Python + GPT Students explore and visualise data with live AI assistance

4. Teamwork & Collaboration

Examples: Group work, interdisciplinary collaboration, feedback

AI Workflows for Team Collaboration
Workflow Tools How it helps
Group ideation boards Lucid + GPT Foster inclusive team brainstorming with shared prompts
Stakeholder simulation ChatGPT roleplay Model different roles and perspectives within a team
Team log summaries ChatGPT + journaling Summarise progress and support shared reflection practices

5. Personal & Professional Development

Examples: Self-management, goal setting, reflective practice

AI Workflows for Professional Growth
Workflow Tools How it helps
Voice-to-plan logs AudioPen, ChatGPT Turn voice notes into organised action plans or reflections
Goal tracking with AI Notion AI, Obsidian + GPT Visualise growth over time and scaffold self-monitoring
AI interview practice ChatGPT, Parrot AI Practise mock interviews and rehearse common scenarios

6. Ethical & Global Citizenship

Examples: Responsible AI use, ethics, cultural awareness

AI Workflows for Ethical Development
Workflow Tools How it helps
Ethics simulation ChatGPT roleplay Explore the consequences of bias, surveillance, and power
Cross-national analysis Claude / GPT + policy docs Compare approaches to global challenges with summarised insights
Sustainability planning ChatGPT + scenario prompts Model low-impact projects with AI co-design support

Guiding Principle: Use AI to scaffold deeper thinking, not shortcut it. Creative workflows allow students to develop their own digital agency and professional identity while practising safe and effective AI use.
