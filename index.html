AI Tools to Support Graduate Capabilities

This GitHub page is also an example of interacting with Codex. Codex is an AI agent that reads instructions from users and automatically updates code.

Microsoft 365 Copilot

Your personal AI assistant built into Word, Excel, and accessible via https://www.microsoft365.com/. Use the Chat feature to break down assignments, summarise documents, plan tasks across weeks, or generate calendar entries. Ideal for improving organisation, task tracking, and planning. Sign in with your @ieg.ac.uk email.

Google Gemini

A powerful general-purpose chatbot for writing help, brainstorming, and task breakdowns. Available at https://gemini.google.com/. Sign in with your IEG account for secure use. Can be paired with Notebook LM and Drive tools.

Lucidchart & Lucidspark

Visual collaboration tools for academic planning, mind maps, diagrams, and group projects. Lucidchart is ideal for structured diagrams like flowcharts and concept maps, while Lucidspark offers a flexible whiteboard for brainstorming and ideation. Both tools are accessible from the same interface once signed in. Sign up with your @ac.uk email at https://lucid.co/lucidspark to unlock a free Educational plan.

Perplexity.ai

A research search engine with real citations. Use it to find recent, peer-reviewed journal articles. Head to https://www.perplexity.ai/. Free accounts have a daily limit, but it's an excellent tool for starting academic research or summarising findings.

Notebook LM

AI workspace for your documents. Upload readings, exemplars, briefs, and mark schemes. Ask questions, generate summaries or outlines, or even produce podcast scripts. Visit https://notebooklm.google/ and sign in with your IEG account.

Suno

AI music generation tool. Create backing tracks, theme songs, or audio branding in seconds. Free credits on sign-up at https://suno.com/.

Adobe Express

Graphic and video design made easy. Ideal for social media assets, posters, and presentations. Access the free tier at https://www.adobe.com/uk/express. Some limits on generative features unless using a paid plan or institutional licence.

Hume AI

Voice and emotion recognition API. Can be used in chatbot projects, affective computing, and feedback loops. Start at https://www.hume.ai/ with free credits for testing.

GitHub Copilot Pro (Education Access)

Code faster with AI suggestions in VS Code and other editors. Educators and students can get Pro access free via the GitHub Education Programme .
