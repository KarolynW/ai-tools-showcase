Step 9: Example Case Study - Fenland Roasters

Context: Fenland Roasters is an independent coffee roaster in Ely, Cambridgeshire. The goal was to develop a digital strategy to improve visibility, engagement, and sales.

Challenges:

  • Limited online visibility
  • Low brand awareness
  • Inconsistent sales
  • Lack of customer engagement

Strategy Highlights:

  1. Website optimisation with e-commerce and subscriptions
  2. Content marketing (blogs, videos, guides)
  3. Social media strategy across Instagram and Facebook
  4. Targeted email marketing with segmentation
  5. Local SEO and Google profile optimisation
  6. Subscription service tiers and user control

Outcomes:

  • Increased visibility and awareness
  • Stable revenue through subscriptions
  • Better engagement and loyalty
  • Established brand authority
